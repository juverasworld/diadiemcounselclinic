Diadem Counselling Clinic is committed to empowering couples and widows in Nigeria through expert guidance, counselling, and supportive programs. We combine professional expertise, empathetic care, and local knowledge to create positive outcomes and lasting change in personal and family wellbeing.

Expert Guidance for Couples and Widows in Nigeria

Led by Professor Florence Eleje Otta, a seasoned counsellor and educator, Diadem Counselling Clinic offers individual and group counselling, seminars, and outreach programs. With years of academic excellence and practical experience, we aim to help Nigerian families navigate marriage challenges and widowhood with confidence and resilience.

Our Mission: Supporting Nigerian Families

To provide accessible, practical, and impactful counselling for couples and widows in Nigeria, enhancing adjustment to life challenges and promoting emotional well-being through seminars, fellowship, and outreach programs.

Our Vision

Diadem Counselling Clinic envisions guiding Nigerian couples to successful marriages and providing support to widows as they navigate loss and transition. Through expert counselling, education, and community programs, we aim to build resilient families and empowered individuals.
