  • Our History

Back In 1980, Anna And David Felt Miles Apart—Emotionally, Even When Sitting Together.

Week by week, guided conversations replaced silence, and understanding replaced resentment. They learned to listen again, share without fear, and see each other with fresh eyes. By the end of their sessions, the distance had softened into closeness. The same couple who once felt like strangers began laughing at the little things, planning weekend walks, and holding hands on the porch just like when they first met.

Work stress, unspoken worries, and years of routine had quietly built a wall between them.

We’ve grown from a small counseling practice in 1980 to a trusted home for lasting emotional support today. Decades of experience have shaped us into a place where couples and individuals keep finding hope and healing.
