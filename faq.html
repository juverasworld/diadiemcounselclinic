  • FAQ’s
  • How do I know if therapy is right for me?
    Therapy can benefit anyone experiencing stress, anxiety, relationship challenges, low mood, or simply seeking personal growth. If you’re unsure, an initial consultation can help you decide.
  • How do I book my first session?
    Booking your first session is simple. You can schedule directly through our online booking system, call our office to speak with our staff, or send us a message through the contact form. We’ll help you find a convenient time for your appointment.
  • Can I stop therapy whenever I want?
    Absolutely. You’re in control of your journey. Some people come for a few weeks, others for months or years — it’s about what feels right for you.
  • Is online therapy as effective as in-person sessions?
    Yes! Research shows that online therapy can be just as effective as face-to-face sessions, with the added convenience of joining from home.
  • What if I don’t click with my therapist?
    A good connection is important. If you don’t feel comfortable, we’ll help you find another therapist who’s a better fit for your needs and personality.
  • Do I need to be going through something big to see a therapist?
    Not at all. You don’t have to wait for a crisis. Therapy can support everyday struggles like stress, confidence, motivation, or simply wanting to better understand yourself.
  • What if I get emotional during a session?
    That’s completely natural. Therapy is a safe space where emotions are welcome — it means you’re processing and healing.
  • What if I’ve never been to therapy before?
    That’s perfectly okay. Many people start therapy feeling unsure — your therapist will guide you step by step and create a safe, welcoming space where you can simply be yourself.
  • Do I need to have a big problem to go to therapy?
    Not at all. Therapy isn’t just for crises — it’s also for stress, self-discovery, or simply having someone to talk to who truly listens.
